How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your Enjoy Baja real estate account directly through the app or by contacting us. Choose the method that works best for you:

Method 1: Delete Through the App (Recommended)

1
Open the App
Launch Enjoy Baja on your device and log into your account
2
Go to Settings
Navigate to your account settings or profile settings
3
Find Data Management
Look for "Data Management" or "Privacy Settings" section
4
Choose Deletion Type
Select either "Delete Specific Data" for partial deletion or "Delete Account" for complete removal
5
Confirm Deletion
Follow the confirmation steps, including password verification and typing "DELETE"
